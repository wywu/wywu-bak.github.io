About Me

I am currently a Ph.D candidate in the Tsinghua National Laboratory for Information Science and Technology (TNList Lab), Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Qiang Zhou. I also work with SenseTime Research. My research interest is in computer vision and machine learning, especially facial landmark detection and adversarial learning. I used to work with Prof. Zhoujun Li during Sep. 2013 to Jun. 2014.
