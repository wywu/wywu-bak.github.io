About Me

Mr. Shuo Yang received his Ph.D. from the department of information engineering, CUHK in July 2017. He is supervised by Prof. Xiaoou Tang and Prof. Chen Change Loy. His research interests include face/object detection and object recognition. Shuo graduated from School of Software, Wuhan University with a B.Eng. in 2013. Then he came to CUHK to continue his study.
